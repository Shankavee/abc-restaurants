Available Services


Table Service
Table service is a global restaurant service where customers are seated at
a table and served by a waiter or waitress.
It involves taking orders, completing food service, and providing assistance during meals,
typically used in formal or upscale restaurants.



Self-service
Self-service restaurants, prevalent in fast-food, cafeterias, and coffee shops,
allow customers to order and pay for food at
a counter or kiosk, enhancing their dining experience. Indonesian restaurant chains,
like Marugame Udon, utilize this concept.
